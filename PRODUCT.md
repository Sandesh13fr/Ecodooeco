# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Six audiences share a single operational workspace, with no single role privileged:

- **Operations / procurement manager** — captures activity data from daily Odoo records without duplicate entry.
- **Finance controller / CFO** — reconciles ESG totals to operational evidence, controlled approval, traceable lineage.
- **ESG / sustainability lead** — builds emission inventories, selects factors, answers disclosures.
- **HR / people lead** — tracks aggregated social metrics, optional employee challenges.
- **SME owner / COO** — wins work via customer questionnaires, reduces costs, sees operating insights.
- **Odoo partner / admin** — installs, configures, and extends modules for clients.

Hackathon presentations also address Odoo judges evaluating product clarity, technical fit, and the trace from operational source to auditable ESG result.

Each audience enters through the same surface but sees views and actions scoped to their role.

## Product Purpose

Enable SMEs to turn approved Odoo operational records into traceable, factor-versioned emissions entries that drive targets and VSME-oriented management reports — without spreadsheets.

## Positioning

Turn an Odoo operational record into a traceable, factor-versioned carbon transaction visible in a goal, dashboard, and one-page ESG summary report.

Ecodoo is the only ESG tool that lives natively inside Odoo — converting a company's existing transactions (Fleet, Purchase, Expense) directly into a traceable, factor-versioned emissions ledger with one-click drill-down from report to source record. Microsoft, SAP, IBM, Workiva, and Sweep all require a separate enterprise data platform to achieve what Ecodoo does inside the system where work already happens.

## Brand Personality

Calm, Technical, Regenerative.

The interface should feel like an authoritative operating system for ESG data — clear, guided, visual, and audit-ready. It earns belief, not excitement. Every screen reinforces that the numbers can be trusted: source provenance, factor lineage, and calculation evidence are never more than one click away.

Visual references are Linear's restraint, Stripe's editorial hierarchy, and Apple's environmental-reporting clarity.

## Anti-references

Ecodoo must never look like a reskinned Odoo admin panel or any of the following:

- **Bloomberg Terminal / SAP GUI** — dense grids of blinking numbers, jargon-heavy labels, information overload, expert-only complexity.
- **Salesforce / HubSpot dashboard clutter** — twelve widgets fighting for attention, none decisive.
- **Consumer "green" apps (Ecosia-style)** — cartoon leaves, hero images of hands holding saplings, feel-good greenwashing that undermines credibility with a CFO.
- **Gamified fitness apps (Strava-style leaderboards)** — competitive ranking, streaks, public scoring applied to employees or ESG performance; feels surveillant and trivializes serious data.
- **Crypto / Web3 dashboards** — neon gradients, animated tickers, "moonshot" energy; the opposite of audit-ready calm.
- **Consultant-style infographic PDFs** — oversized circular charts, pastel gradients, stock photography standing in for actual traceable data.
- **Generic sustainability marketing** — purple SaaS gradients, excessive glassmorphism, green globes, hands holding saplings, leaf-covered dashboards, and greenwashing language.

The underlying rule: if it looks like it's trying to impress or excite, it's wrong. Ecodoo should look like it's trying to be **believed**.

## Design Principles

1. **Trace before spectacle.** Every number drills to source, unit conversion, factor, factor version, formula, approver, and correction history. The evidence is never more than one click away.

2. **Odoo-native by default.** Reuse authentication, ORM, multi-company, mail, activities, views, reports, and source modules. Do not invent parallel infrastructure.

3. **Privacy by design.** Aggregate social metrics. Make individual challenges and leaderboards opt-in. Treat diversity, participation, and policy-acknowledgement data as sensitive.

4. **Audit-ready calm.** Clear hierarchy, controlled visual density, predictable navigation. The interface earns trust through transparency and restraint, not decoration or urgency.

5. **One golden path before breadth.** Transaction → activity → factor → emissions ledger → target/score → report/action. Nail this flow before expanding to secondary paths.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction standards.
- Never use color as the sole indicator; pair with text, icons, or patterns.
- Support reduced motion — all animations degrade gracefully to instant transitions.
- Design for color blindness: semantic states carry more than hue changes.
- Keyboard-reachable navigation and focus order that matches visual hierarchy.
- Empty, error, loading, and permission-denied states on every surface.
- Use semantic HTML, responsive layouts, and visible keyboard focus.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences and never autoplay audio.
- Video may support a message but must never carry essential information alone.
